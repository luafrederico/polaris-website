About Us


We always search for a better way to live our own life so, we decided to show how to create homes with a advanced technology and sustainable.

Polaris is the name given to our web site because our intention was to show two "poles", the negative side of the construction of large houses and then, its positive side: its possibility to be totally sustainable and technological.

Our great passion for technology and great architectural constructions along with the great demand for sustainable solutions in the market were the reasons for the construction of our empire, Polaris.
Born in 2018, GQL ia a new group in web development world.
Carlos, Gabriela, Gabriel, Luana and Queila builded all from zero.
Students in "Instituto Federal de São Paulo", decided to open this startup for a job in class, but they didn't imagined the sucess this could do!

The light. Our logo.


The light. The symbol of great ideas. Ideas with great potentials capable of changing the world we live in. It means energy, technology. The colors symbolize sustainability and the connection with nature.
